An Unforgettable night at San Siro

On June of 2023, I had the privilege of watching an AC Milan match that was nothing short of a spectacle. The San Siro stadium was buzzing with energy, the air filled with the chants of die-hard Rossoneri fans. As the AC Milan players took to the pitch, each stride resonated with the hopes and dreams of thousands. The match was a thrilling display of football, with our team showcasing their prowess and strategy. There were moments that took our breath away, near misses that had us on the edge of our seats, and a triumphant goal in the dying minutes that will be etched in our memories forever. The roar of the crowd when the final whistle blew was a testament to the love for this beautiful game. It was not just a match, it held a farewell of one of our legends Zalatan Ibrahimovic, all fans were chanting his name for a last time in their home and in football generally. It was a testament of Rossoneri sad and happy emotions.

Japan’s Majestic Mountains

Japan is a country rich in mountains, with around three quarters of the country covered by them1. The most iconic of them all is Mount Fuji, standing at 3,776m, and is the tallest mountain in Japan. Other notable mountains include Mount Tsukuba, known for its beauty and hiking trails, and Mount Ontake, the second tallest volcano in Japan.

Venice: The City of Water and Art

Venice is a city of beauty, history, and culture. It is built on a group of small islands connected by bridges and canals, and has been a major maritime and financial power since the Middle Ages. Venice is famous for its artistic and architectural heritage, such as the Doge’s Palace, St. Mark’s Basilica, and the Peggy Guggenheim Collection.
